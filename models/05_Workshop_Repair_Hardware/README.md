# Category 05 — Workshop, Repair & Hardware

General-purpose workshop hardware: brackets, clips, trays, and cable management.

## Material Recommendation
PETG for most workshop applications. Higher infill (50%+) for structural brackets.

## Print Settings — Structural Parts
- Minimum 4 perimeters on brackets
- 40–50% infill for load-bearing clips and brackets
- Gyroid infill for multi-directional strength

## Included STL Files
- `l_bracket_30x30x3mm.stl` — 30×30mm L-bracket, 3mm walls, 25mm deep
- `cable_clip_5mm_id.stl` — U-channel clip for 5mm OD wire/cable
- `parts_tray_50x30x8.stl` — Open-top workshop parts tray
- `zip_tie_anchor.stl` — Two-post zip-tie loop anchor base
