# Category 03 — Garden & Greenhouse

Low-stakes, high-volume items for garden infrastructure. Most items here are
"print and replace" consumables — inexpensive PETG works well and lasts 3–5 seasons.

## Material Recommendation
- Film clips, hoop clips: **PETG** (UV stable, flexible enough for snap-fit)
- Soil stakes: **PETG** (resists moisture in soil)
- Plant labels: PETG or ASA (write with UV-permanent Sharpie or laser-engrave)

## Included STL Files
- `greenhouse_film_clip_16mm.stl` — Film clip for 16mm EMT conduit hoops
- `row_cover_hoop_clip_9mm.stl` — End clip for 9mm row-cover hoop
