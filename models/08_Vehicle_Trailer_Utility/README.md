# Category 08 — Vehicle & Trailer Utility

Truck, trailer, and ATV accessories for off-grid operations.

## ⚠️ Safety Notices
- Trailer jack pads are for stabiliser/tongue jacks **only** — never under a lifting jack
- No 3D-printed parts in trailer hitch couplers, safety chain brackets, or brake lines
- Organizer clips are not cargo tie-down components

## Material Recommendation
PETG minimum (temperature resistance needed in vehicle environments).
ASA for any parts exposed to direct sun in a truck bed.

## Included STL Files
- `trailer_jack_foot_pad_120mm.stl` — 120mm wide jack foot pad for soft ground
- `ratchet_strap_hook_keeper.stl` — Keeps strap hooks captured when stored
