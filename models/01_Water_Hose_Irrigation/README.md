# Category 01 — Water, Hose & Irrigation

## ⚠️ Safety Notice
**NO model in this category is rated for pressurized water lines.**
- Gravity-fed drip systems (< 5 PSI) only
- Rain barrel gravity distribution only
- Hose guides and clips — hose is not under pressure while clipped

For all pressurized (mains) plumbing: use certified plumbing hardware only.

## Material Recommendation
**PETG minimum** for all outdoor water components. ASA for parts exposed to prolonged direct sunlight.
Replace any parts showing UV whitening, cracking, or significant deformation.
