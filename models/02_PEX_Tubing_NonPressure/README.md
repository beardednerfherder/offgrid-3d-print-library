# Category 02 — PEX Tubing (Non-Pressure Applications Only)

## ⚠️ Safety Notice
**Absolutely no pressurized water lines.** Models in this category are for:
- Gravity drainage (grey water, condensate, overflow)
- Organizational strap clips to support tubing runs
- Non-load-bearing support brackets

Never use 3D-printed parts in domestic hot water, potable water, or any
pressurized line regardless of filament choice.

## Material Recommendation
PETG for indoor or sheltered use. ASA for any outdoor or UV-exposed runs.

## Included STL Files
- `pex_support_clip_12mm.stl` — Strap support clip for 12mm OD PEX drainage tubing
