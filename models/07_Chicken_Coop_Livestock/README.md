# Category 07 — Chicken Coop & Livestock

Coop hardware, feed accessories, and small-livestock infrastructure.

## ⚠️ Safety Notices
- **Coop latches are not predator-proof** — use metal hardware for predator exclusion
- Heat lamp proximity: **ASA or ABS only**. PLA and PETG are fire hazards near heat
- No 3D-printed components in waterers without food-safe filament certification
- Inspect all coop parts every 3–6 months for UV degradation; replace brittle items

## Material Recommendation
ASA for anything outdoors or near heat sources. PETG for sheltered coop interior items.

## Included STL Files
- `j_hook_fence_hanger.stl` — J-hook for fence/coop wire hanging
- `feed_supplement_tray_80x50.stl` — Small open-top supplement tray
