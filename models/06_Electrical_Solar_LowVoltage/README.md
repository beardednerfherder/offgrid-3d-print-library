# Category 06 — Electrical, Solar & Low-Voltage DC

12V/24V solar systems, Meshtastic nodes, sensors, wire management.

## ⚠️ Safety Notices
- **Never** use 3D-printed parts on or near mains (120V/240V AC) circuits
- Battery terminal covers are safety items — print at 60%+ infill, verify fit
- MC4 covers are dust protection only — de-energize before connect/disconnect
- Solar clips are retention aids, not structural mounts for high-wind installations

## Material Recommendation
**ASA preferred** for any outdoor solar/comms installation.
PETG acceptable for sheltered or indoor low-voltage work.

## Included STL Files
- `wire_wall_clip_3mm.stl` — Wall-mount clip for 3mm wire with mounting tab
- `solar_panel_corner_clip.stl` — Corner retention clip for solar panels
- `conduit_saddle_16mm_emt.stl` — Wall saddle for ½″ EMT conduit
